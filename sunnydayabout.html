Our History, Our Mission:

Established in 2008, Sunny Bakery began in Dekalb, IL in the basement of the Treehouse Cafe. The mission was simple, to bake the best cookie. We embrace our roots in Subculture and hold true to our standards in organic indgredients. From our family to yours, we look forward to providing the best cookies to you and your customers! - Chuck and Tony
